ATS Terminal Desktop Application

ATS Terminal Desktop is an advanced Smart TV recovery tool that helps fix logo-hang issues and open Recovery Mode with a single click, making Smart TV servicing faster, easier, and more reliable.

Android App Controller Desktop Application

Android App Controller Desktop lets you remotely install and uninstall both third-party and default apps on Android TVs, providing a fast and efficient solution for Android TV app management.

Mstar Dump Unpacker

MStar Dump Unpacker quickly unpacks MStar eMMC dump files with a single click, allowing technicians to access and modify firmware files easily for repair and customization.

ATS Terminal Android App

ATS Terminal Android App allows technicians to connect to Android TVs via Bluetooth from their mobile phone to recover logo-hang issues, open Recovery Mode with one click, and easily modify TV service parameters.

ATS Service Remote Android App

ATS Service Remote Android App helps technicians quickly access Android TV service modes and easily modify service parameters from their mobile phone, providing a fast and convenient solution for Smart TV servicing

ATS Tech Hub Android App

ATS Tech Hub is a Smart TV repair learning app that offers video tutorials, repair lessons, official firmware downloads, and T-CON division firmware, providing technicians with everything they need for Smart TV mainboard repair in one place.
